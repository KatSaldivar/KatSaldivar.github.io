Simon Game | July 2017

Recreated a virtual version of the classic Simon game where users are presented with a random series of button presses that they need to remember. Each time they input a series of button presses correctly, they see the same series of button presses but with an additional step. The user will hear a sound that corresponds to each button both when the series of button presses plays, and when the user presses a button, as well as a counter to see what level they have reached, to create a more fluid user experience. If the user presses the wrong button, they notified and that series of button presses starts again to remind them of the correct pattern or they can press the button to play in strict mode where if they get a button press wrong, the game restarts at a new random series of button presses.

Star Wars Tic Tac Toe | July 2017

Pomodoro Clock | June 2017

Unicorn Calculator | June 2017

This calculator additionally has the functionality to clear the input field with the push of a button and to keep chaining mathematical operations together until the equal button is hit, and the calculator will give the correct output.
